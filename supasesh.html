Built With

Interaction Focus

The product hinges on direct manipulation. You should be able to drag sessions, resize them fluidly, and reorganize a day without being pushed through forms for every small adjustment.

Product Logic

  • Blocks carry state as movable, editable time ranges
  • Schedule summaries update immediately as the timeline changes
  • The same metaphor has to hold across phone, desktop, and spatial layouts

Native App Intent

The actual product is built for the Apple ecosystem and uses native storage and UI frameworks. The design goal is not novelty for its own sake; it is making session planning feel tactile and continuous.

Why This Embed Works

A browser port of the timeline interaction communicates the app far better than screenshots. Users can immediately understand the scheduling model that makes SupaSesh different from a static calendar layout.
