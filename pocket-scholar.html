Sub-Stepping & Stability

Pool breaks involve a high density of simultaneous collisions. In naive collision engines, moving balls large distances per frame overlap and inject unrealistic energy into the system.

Pocket Scholar resolves motion in multiple passes per frame, then layers motion trails and spin readouts on top so the simulation looks clearer without changing the underlying math.
