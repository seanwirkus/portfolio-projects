Sub-Stepping & Stability

Pool breaks involve a high density of simultaneous collisions. In naive collision engines, moving balls large distances per frame causes them to overlap, generating "explosive" unrealistic energy.

Pocket Scholar uses iterative sub-stepping, solving constraints multiple times per visual frame to lock the table state accurately.
