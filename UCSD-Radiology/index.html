Our culture of compassionate, patient-centered care; innovative teaching; and mentorship by world-renowned faculty in state-of-the-art imaging facilities makes UCSD Radiology the ideal environment for learners, investigators, and those seeking expert diagnostic services.

Interventional Radiology

Transforming Care with Minimally Invasive Techniques
Interventional Radiology (IR) is a specialized field that focuses on diagnosing and treating patients using the least invasive techniques available. By employing advanced technologies, we guide minimally invasive procedures that significantly reduce risks and improve patient outcomes.

Our team of experts is dedicated to providing innovative solutions for a wide range of medical conditions, ensuring optimal patient outcomes with reduced recovery times.

Precision Medicine Meets Cutting-Edge Technology

Advancing Precision Diagnostics & Personalized Therapies
At UC San Diego Radiology, we’re leveraging state-of-the-art technologies to sharpen both diagnosis and treatment. By integrating advanced imaging methods and data-driven tools, we’re able to detect disease earlier, tailor interventions more precisely, and monitor progress in real time.

These breakthroughs don’t just improve efficiency—they expand access and personalize care, ensuring every patient benefits from the most effective, least-invasive options available.
