DNA Replication Study

An interactive study tool for DNA replication: explore the replication fork with labeled enzymes, learn bond types, and test your knowledge with quiz modes covering enzymes and key concepts.

Embedded here: an interactive runtime with a fork diagram, enzyme detail cards, concept cards, and two quiz modes.

Core concept Replication fork anatomy

Interactive diagram showing leading strand, lagging strand, Okazaki fragments, and all key enzymes in their positions.

Learning mode Click-to-explore enzymes

Click any enzyme on the diagram or sidebar to see its function, bond type, location, and whether it's used in mismatch repair.

Quiz modes Enzymes & Concepts

Two quiz modes test enzyme identification and conceptual understanding of bonds, directionality, and processes.

Launch the direct runtime for the full interactive experience.

Tech Stack

JavaScript Canvas Molecular Biology Education UX

What It Covers

All enzymes at the replication fork (helicase, primase, Pol III, Pol I, ligase, topoisomerase, SSB, telomerase), phosphodiester vs hydrogen bonds, leading vs lagging synthesis, Okazaki fragments, Pol III dual activities, endo vs exonucleases, 5-methylcytosine, and mismatch repair.

Why It Matters

  • Maps each enzyme to its position on the fork
  • Connects enzyme function to specific bond chemistry
  • Quiz modes reinforce memorization of key concepts
